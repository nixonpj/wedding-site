## Accommodation and Venue

### Where is the venue?

[Amita Rasa](https://amitarasa.com) is a dedicated wedding venue spread over 28 acres. The venue is 1.5 hours away from Bangalore in the getaway spot of Nandi Hills.

### Where can I stay?

We've arranged for a block of rooms at three hotels near the venue. Please fill out the [RSVP form](/rsvp/) and we will book your stay for the 26th and 27th at one of the hotels.

### What is the check-in/check-out time?

Standard check-in is 2:00 PM and check-out is 11:00 AM. You can re-fill the [RSVP form](/rsvp/) with details of your travel once confirmed, and we will try our best to work with the hotels to accommodate any out-of-band arrivals or departures.

### Will the ceremonies be indoors or outdoors?

Amita Rasa is mostly an outdoor venue. We will have covered pavilions for most of the ceremonies.

---

## Travel

### Will there be transportation provided?

Shuttle services will be available from the hotels to the venue for the convenience of guests staying at the hotels. Please indicate your transportation needs when you [RSVP](/rsvp/).

### What time should I arrive?

We recommend arriving on the morning of the 26th in Bangalore for guests traveling from outside Bangalore. For Bangalore residents, we recommend making a day trip out of the wedding.

### How do I get to the venue?

The venue is located at Nandi Hills, Bangalore. Detailed directions and a map will be provided after you [RSVP](/rsvp/), based on where you are traveling from. The venue is approximately 60 kilometers from Bangalore city center.

---

## Food

### Will there be vegetarian options?

All meals from lunch on the 26th to breakfast on the 28th will be catered by us. There will only be vegetarian options during the course of the two-day wedding for religious reasons.

### Can you accommodate dietary restrictions or allergies?

We want to make sure everyone can enjoy the celebration. Please let us know about any dietary restrictions, allergies, or special requirements when you [RSVP](/rsvp/), and we will let you know if we cannot accommodate your request.
